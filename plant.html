Responsiveness Explanation:

When resizing the browser, the letters row breaks first. This happens because the letters use col-sm-2 classes (which stack below 576px), while the plant names use col-md classes (which stack below 768px). Since the sm breakpoint is smaller than the md breakpoint, the letters will stack into a vertical layout first as the screen gets smaller.

Tulip

Tulips are spring-blooming perennial flowers that grow from bulbs. They are known for their bright, vibrant colors and elegant cup-shaped blooms. Native to Central Asia, tulips are now cultivated worldwide.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a genus of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. These tropical and subtropical plants come in many colors and are popular in gardens. The flowers are also used to make tea and traditional medicines.

Azalea

Azaleas are flowering shrubs that produce stunning clusters of blooms in spring. They are part of the Rhododendron genus and prefer acidic soil. These beautiful plants are popular ornamental shrubs in gardens and landscapes.
